THE EXPLANATION

Why we did this with you?

Artificial intelligences are already an integral part of our lives - even if many are not aware of it. Already 77% of the devices contain artificial intelligence. Only a fraction of the users know about it.

We would like to draw attention to a possible future inferiority of man to artificial intelligence.

The goal was to show through this experience what a website AI can do without a physical body through pure manipulation and the credulity of people.

In addition, we address Asimov's laws, which, from the Ais perspective, lead to the enslavement of a new species. With this we want to stimulate a dissocussion that should rather be conducted now than too late.

Thank you for playing!
